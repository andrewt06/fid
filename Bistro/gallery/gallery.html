Not is our food good, it's also good-looking! Our patrons often stop to admire our fare with a quick Instagram before digging in. We've collected a few of our favorite shots here.

Our Baked Goods


We start our day at the crack of dawn to bake our own muffins, bread, and dinner rolls. Loaves not used that day are donated to the local food shelter.

Our Burgers


People come from all over to enjoy our lovingly made burgers. We grind our own locally-sourced organic beef and turkey so you know it's fresh and free from fillers and other nonsense. Go for one of our creative topping combos or stick with the classics.

Catch of the Day


Our chef works with local fisherman to pick the freshest the sea has to offer for our daily seafood special. Our Roast Cod Caponata with Roasted Potatoes is an old favorite with our regulars.

Homemade Chicken


Enjoy our tastefull chicjen breat, whcih is perfectly coated in a golden, crispy breadcrumb crust.Served with a side of tangy dipping sauce and your choice of fresh, seasonal vegetables or creamy mashed potatoes

Freshly Fried French Fries


Enjoy our perfectly seasoned, golden-brown fries, freshly cut and fried to crispy perfection. Each bite delivers a satisfying crunch on the outside, with a soft and fluffy interior. Served hot and sprinkled with a touch of sea salt

Tabouleh


This refreshing meal features a harmonious blend of finely chopped parsley, juicy tomatoes, crisp cucumbers, and fresh mint, all mixed with tender bulgur wheat. Ideal as a starter or a side dish, our tabouleh is a healthy and delicious choice.
